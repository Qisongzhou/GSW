Who we are

The Golden State Warriors are a professional basketball team based in San Francisco, California. They are a part of the Western Conference Pacific Division in the National Basketball Association (NBA).

The team was originally established in 1946 as the Philadelphia Warriors, based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, where the franchise won the championship in the inaugural season of the Basketball Association of America (BAA), the league that became the NBA after a merger in 1949. The Warriors later moved to the San Francisco Bay area in 1962 and were renamed the Golden State Warriors in 1971.
